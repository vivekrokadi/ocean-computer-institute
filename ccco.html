CCCO (Certificate Course in Computer Operations)

Learn the basics of computer operations, MS Office, and internet usage for personal and professional growth.

Course Duration: 3 Months

What You'll Learn:

  • Computer Fundamentals: Hardware, software, and operating systems.
  • MS Office: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and basic documentation skills.
  • Internet & Email: Browsing, search engines, and email communication.
  • Digital Transactions: Online banking, UPI, and cybersecurity awareness.
  • Basic Typing Skills: Improve typing speed and accuracy.
  • Introduction to IT: Basics of networking and digital literacy.
  • Practical Assignments: Real-world exercises to enhance learning.
