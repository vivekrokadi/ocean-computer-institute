ACO (Advanced Computer Operations)

Enhance your computer skills with advanced office applications, data management, and IT fundamentals for professional growth.

Course Duration: 3 Months

What You'll Learn:

  • Advanced MS Office: Master Excel, Word, and PowerPoint for business applications.
  • Database Management: Learn basics of SQL and database handling.
  • IT Fundamentals: Networking basics, cybersecurity, and troubleshooting.
  • Digital Workplace Skills: Cloud computing, remote collaboration, and automation tools.
  • Internet & Security: Secure browsing, ethical hacking basics, and online safety.
  • Practical Assignments: Hands-on exercises to implement learning.
