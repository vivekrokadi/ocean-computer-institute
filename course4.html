Computer Teacher Training Course (CTTC)

Gain the skills needed to become a professional computer instructor. Learn advanced computer applications, teaching techniques, and educational tools.

Course Duration: 1.5 Years

What You'll Learn:

  • Fundamentals: Basics of computers, operating systems, and file management.
  • MS Office: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook for educational use.
  • Programming Basics: Introduction to Python, C, and JavaScript.
  • Web Technologies: HTML, CSS, and basic website development.
  • Teaching Methodologies: Classroom management, lesson planning, and student engagement techniques.
  • Educational Technology: Using online learning tools and digital resources.
  • Practical Assignments: Hands-on teaching practice and real-world scenarios.
  • Final Project: Prepare and deliver a complete computer course module. And much more
