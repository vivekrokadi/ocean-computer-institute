Tally Prime

Gain expertise in Tally Prime for financial management, GST with practical applications.

Course Duration:2 Months

What You'll Learn:

  • Basics of Tally Prime: Introduction, installation, and overview of features.
  • Company Creation: Setting up a company, managing multiple companies, and configuring basic settings.
  • Accounting Masters: Ledger and group creation, chart of accounts, and voucher types.
  • Inventory Management: Stock groups, items, units of measure, and inventory voucher types.
  • GST in Tally: Setting up GST, creating GST-compliant invoices, and filing GST returns.
  • Payroll Management: Creating employee records, salary details, and payroll reports.
  • Banking Features: Managing bank accounts, reconciliation, and generating payment vouchers.
  • Reports and Analysis: Generating financial statements, stock summary, GST reports, and ratio analysis.
  • Advanced Features: Security controls, data backup, restore, and synchronization.
  • Project: Create a mock company to manage accounts, inventory, GST, and payroll end-to-end.
