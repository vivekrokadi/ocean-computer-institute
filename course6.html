Diploma in Office Assistance

A comprehensive program covering office management tools, MS Office for office work efficiency.

Course Duration: 8 Months

What You'll Learn:

  • Introduction to Office Automation: Overview, purpose, and scope of automation in the workplace.
  • Computer Basics: Fundamentals of hardware, software, and operating systems.
  • Microsoft Word: Document creation, formatting, tables, mail merge, and templates.
  • Microsoft Excel: Data management, formulas, functions, charts, pivot tables, and financial templates.
  • Microsoft PowerPoint: Designing effective presentations, using animations, transitions, and slide templates.
  • Microsoft Access: Basics of databases, creating tables, forms, queries, and reports.
  • Internet Basics: Browsing, email communication, and working with cloud storage like Google Drive or OneDrive.
  • File Management: Organizing folders, compressing files, and data backup procedures.
  • Basic Accounting with Tally Prime: Managing ledgers, vouchers, GST compliance, and generating financial reports.
  • Advanced Features: Introduction to collaborative tools (Microsoft Teams/Office 365) and basic troubleshooting techniques.
  • Project: Create a comprehensive project integrating word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, and basic accounting for a mock organization.
