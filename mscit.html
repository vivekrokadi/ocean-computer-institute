MS-CIT

Gain essential computer skills, IT knowledge, and digital literacy to enhance your career opportunities.

Course Duration: 3 Months

What You'll Learn:

  • Computer Basics: Understanding hardware, software, and OS.
  • MS Office Suite: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook.
  • Internet & Email: Browsing, online security, and email management.
  • Digital Transactions: Online banking, UPI, and e-commerce basics.
  • Cyber Security: Safe internet practices and data protection.
  • IT Awareness: Cloud computing, social media, and digital transformation.
  • Practical Projects: Hands-on assignments for real-world application.
